    class Adjustment

    Adjustments are modifiers affecting the price of an adjustable object. Anything adjustable may have an adjustment that adds its amount to the price of the adjustable. The adjustable must respond to methods tax_rate and price_includes_tax? so that the adjustable can return its amount with or without tax. The attached label provides detailed information. If an adjustment has an associated source, it should respond to `description` that defines the contents of the label.
